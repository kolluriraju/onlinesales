About the Land.com Network

The Land.com Network features the largest and most effective marketplaces
to discover, buy and sell rural real estate.

Our Network Includes:

 

Leading Rural Real Estate Listing Sites

Lands of America, Land And Farm and LandWatch connect more than 7 million active buyers and land owners with the best local real estate professionals every month. These premier rural property listing sites drive top search engine visibility and high-quality traffic online and from mobile devices.

Properties listed include farms, ranches, timberland, hunting land, mountain property, lake houses, river homes, beachfront homes and country homes on acreage.

LAND Magazines

LAND and Texas LAND magazines are the largest rural property publications in the country. Reaching over 240,000 high-end readers and land enthusiasts each year, the quarterly magazines highlight luxury properties as well as top brokers, agents and sellers.

40+ Partner Sites

To maximize exposure for both land sellers and buyers, listings in our network are syndicated to targeted websites with audiences interested in rural real estate. Ranging from publications and associations to lifestyle sites, our partners include AgWeb, equine.com, REALTORS Land Institute and many more.

 

Land.com, Lands of America, Land And Farm, LandWatch and LAND Magazines are owned by the Costar Group (Nasdaq: CSGP).
